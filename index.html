Annual Report 2013

A look back at our most rewarding year so far.

A Look Back

We’d like to take a moment and thank you for your involvement with the Columbia Organization and Rising Entrepreneurs. CORE has grown exponentially this past year, and we’ve reached 2000 subscribers to our newsletter, 2000+ Facebook likes, and almost 700 Twitter followers. Whether you have subscribed to our newsletter, attended our events, partnered with us for our programs, spoken at our events, gotten involved with our competitions, or supported us in any other way, we could not have come this far without you. We launched over 87 new programs and event series this year, and we’d like to look back at what we have accomplished together


educate

We hold speaker sessions and founder panels, educating students about everything from VC funding to coding their intial MVPs. We work with other campus organizations to co-host educational workshops designed to educate the next generation of student entrepreneurs.

inspire

We've brought speakers like Jack Dorsey (Founder of Twitter and Square), Jessica Livingston (Founder and Partner at YCombinator) and Drew Houston (Founder of Dropbox) amongst others to campus to share their founder stories.

launch

We've held competitions like the CORE Product Challenge to guide students through the product development cycle. We've also worked with Columbia Entrepreneurship and CEO to hold Columbia's inaugral entrepreneurship festival, in which we gave away $50,000 in award money.

A look back at our tremendous growth in the span of one academic year
